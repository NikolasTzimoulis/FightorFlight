Text Field:
	Type <taskname> <minutes> in the text field then press ENTER to create new pledge. A Task Progressbar will show up.
	Press UP or DOWN arrows in the text field to get suggestions from pledges that occured around the same time in previous days.
	Double left-click in the text field to view incomplete Recent Tasks.
	Double right-click i nthe text field to view completed Recent Tasks.
Recent Tasks:
	Double left-click on a task to re-schedule its last successful pledge in the Text Field.
	Triple middle-click on a task to delete the last successful pledge of this task.
Time Window Button:
	Left or right-click the button on the bottom right to change the time window. The number shown is in days.
Last Pledge:
	On your bottom left, you can see the number of successful pledges for the last two time windows.
	Double left-click on the Last Pledge to get a chart of successful pledges from further back.
	Double left-click on the Last Pledge to mark the task temporarily complete. (use the Text Field to designate a non-default expiration in hours)
	Triple left-click on the Last Pledge to mark the task complete forever.
Pledge Progressbar:
	Progress bar shows how close you are to the end of the pledge.
	Double click anywhere on the Pledge Progressbar to re-schedule the pledge in the Text Field.
	Once the time is up, the progressbar will be replaced by buttons, so you can choose whether the pledge was successful.
